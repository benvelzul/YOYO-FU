## About the Project

This game is inspired by **Boomerang Fu**, one of my favorite games of all time, especially because of how simple but entretaining it is. It's also inspired by the physics of how a yoyo works, it has always amazed me how much stuff you can do with a yoyo or just a string. 
The gameplay is like this:
You start with a yoyo with no [power-ups](#power-ups) or [tricks](#tricks) you can get those by collecting the books (for tricks) and the orbs (for power-ups). 
The only damage the yoyo does is push the player and give a little cooldown to the player that is attacked (victim). This give the othe player to opportunity to wrap the victim with the yoyo and eleminate it from that round (the control for wraping and trowing is the same, you just repeat it). 
The victim can also be eliminated through pushes until it falls into the void, or into the water. And through power ups or tricks.

## Features

* **Physics-Driven Combat:** Master the unique string-tether dynamics, momentum, and recoil of the yoyo.
* **Tricks System:** Collect **Books** scattered across the arena to unlock specialized yoyo techniques and moves to have more ways to trap and attack your opponents.
* **Power-ups:** Grab **Orbs** to imbue your yoyo with game-changing active and passive abilities.
* **Hazardous Arenas:** Fight on dynamic stages where the environment (voids, water) is just as dangerous as the players, there are also moving arenas where you will have to donge crushing wall while fighting with your opponents.
